Founders Story

The 'story' part of Storyblok

Like all great ideas, Storyblok started as a spark of inspiration.

Founders Dominik and Alexander were working for a digital experience agency when one day they started to scratch their heads and wonder: why the heck is content management so clunky and slow and annoying? 📦 With their developer hats on, they started to work on a way to solve the day-to-day problems they were having.

And then one day, out popped a prototype for a Headless CMS. They named it Storyblok. And like all proud parents, they loved and nurtured and developed it from that day forward.

Born in Austria, Storyblok is a CMS that was built for the world 🌏. And as more and more customers and fellow Storyblokers have flocked to get in on the fun, it has grown and grown into the feature-rich, enterprise-ready content management system that devs and marketers love so much today.

Our mission

Ship great content

Shipping great content should be fun. So we want to revolutionize content management for mid-market and enterprise businesses by creating a seamless experience that both dev and marketing teams love.

People first

We're a 'people-first' company. So the cutting-edge features we introduce are designed to make the people who use Storyblok's lives easier. By accelerating the whole content life cycle. By streamlining content workflows. And by making friction-free collaboration between developer and marketing teams possible.

Flexible remote

Our people are the foundation of our mission – we are dedicated to nurturing an inclusive, collaborative and flexible remote working environment that fosters high performing teams, empowering us to scale to $100m ARR and beyond.
